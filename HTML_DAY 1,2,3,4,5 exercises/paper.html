BREAKING: Local Tech Hub Launches New Campus

Startup community celebrates expanded facilities and workforce growth

The new campus opened its doors on Monday, welcoming over 1,000 visitors and promising expanded collaboration spaces for startups and developers. Officials say the move will create hundreds of jobs and attract talent from across the region.

Programs at the campus will include mentorship for young founders, a co-working zone, and regular hackathons. Organizers expect partnerships with universities and industry leaders to accelerate product development and community learning.
